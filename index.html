Eren Yeager is a character from the anime and manga series "Attack on Titan". He possesses the ability to transform into a Titan, which grants him immense strength and regenerative abilities. Eren's Titan form is known as the Attack Titan, and it allows him to fight against other Titans and protect humanity.
